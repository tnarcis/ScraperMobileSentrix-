üéØ What is MobileSentrix Extractor?

MobileSentrix Extractor is a powerful web scraping tool designed for TXParts team to automatically extract product pricing data from mobile parts suppliers. It fetches product information, applies custom discounts, and exports data in various formats for easy analysis.

üí° Pro Tip

This tool supports multiple websites including MobileSentrix CA/COM, XCellParts, and TXParts. It automatically detects the site and uses the appropriate scraper engine!

üöÄ Results Dashboard & Auto-Scraper

Use the Results page to run scheduled-quality scrapes with live progress. The workflow is the same for all three supported clients and keeps runs lightweight by default so you get actionable results quickly.

  1. Select a client: MobileSentrix (default), XCell Parts, or TXParts. The toggle keeps the UI in sync and updates available categories.
  2. Choose depth: Max pages per category defaults to 5 for faster runs. Set to 0 to crawl full depth when you have time.
  3. Pick categories (optional): Use the drawer to target specific brands/models. Leaving it empty scans every discovered category.
  4. Launch the scrape: Tap Run Scrape. The job card shows pages, items, categories, ETA, and any automatic limits engaged.
  5. Monitor progress: Open the Progress modal for real-time stats. Finished runs appear in the dashboard cards immediately.

‚ö†Ô∏è Auto-Cap Safety

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How many pages should I scrape?

A: Default is 10 pages (max 20). Most categories have all important products in the first 5-10 pages. Going higher causes timeouts.

Q: Why are some products missing?

A: Check your filters! You might have price range or keyword filters that exclude those products. Also, out-of-stock items might not be scraped.

Q: Can I scrape multiple sites at once?

A: Yes! Just paste URLs from different sites (MobileSentrix, XCellParts, TXParts). The tool automatically detects and uses the right scraper for each.

Q: How do I export just my watchlist?

A: Star the items you want, then check "Show watchlist ‚òÖ only" checkbox. All export buttons will then export only starred items.

Q: What's the difference between CSV and XLSX?

A: XLSX has better formatting, preserves data types, and can be opened directly in Excel. CSV is simpler and works with any spreadsheet software.

Q: My scrape is taking too long. What to do?

A: Reduce max pages to 5-7, use fewer URLs per batch, or check your internet connection. If still slow, try again later (the site might be slow).
